Air fryer Vegan Sweet Potato Fritters

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup finely diced onion
  • 1/2 tablesppon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teasppon ground pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground tumeric
  • avocado oil cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat airfryer to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine shredded sweet potato, almond floud, onions, olive oil, salt, pepper and tumeric in a bowl; mix until well combined.
  3. Scoop into 9 balls and form patties.
  4. Place into air fryer without patties touching each other and spray with cooking spray
  5. Cook in air fryer until edges are browned, about 10 minutes
  6. Flip and apply cooking spray. Cook another 6 minutes
  7. Rest for 2 minutes before removing from basket and serve.
