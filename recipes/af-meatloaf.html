Air Fryer Meatloaf

Air fryer meatloaf topped with scallions and a side of mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 mushrooms, thickly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Ground pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat airfryer to 392 degrees F
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, bread crumbs, thyme, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Knead and mix thoroughly. Transfer mixture to a small loaf pan. Press in mushrooms and coat with olive oil.
  3. Roast meatloaf in airfryer until nicely browned, about 25 minutes. Check internal temperature at center to be at least 165 degrees F
  4. Remove and allow meatloaf to rest 10 minutes before slicing and serving
